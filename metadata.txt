Welcome to Fzofil Diary : The ultimate movie tracking app
With Fzofil Diary you can effortlessly manage your movie watchlist and keep track of the movies you've watched.
Create diary and notes with beautiful icons, you can choose many cute icons according to your feeling

FEATURES
- Elegant and simple UI for manage diary & note.
- Track and discover Movies and TV Shows
- Create note or diary easily with emotional icons, so you can save information about your favorite movie & show
- Attach image to your note, along with location, and rate the movie by your self
- Statistics of watched movies in graph form with icons showing your feelings and reviews about the movie & TV Show
